# Oracle Argus Safety On-Premises to Safety One (SaaS) upgrade
-   Status: proposed
-   Deciders: Alexander Baumann
-   Date: 2026-06-04

## Platform Name
CH Data Assets, Analytics & AI

## Demand Type
Strategic Demand

## Description
To ensure Business continuity and vendor support, a 5-year contract to Upgrade Oracle Argus on-Prem to Oracle Safety One (SaaS) is required.

Argus is a highly business critical application used in the business process "Pharmacovigilance Single Case Processing", to report adverse events about Bayer pharmaceutical products to global health authorities.
A risk of failing to meet the reporting timelines for adverse event information to the authorities can in the worst case put the marketing authorization for Bayer's pharmaceutical products at risk.
Argus onPrem is running out of support and a successor system is needed for implementation well-in advance

## Objectives
To ensure Business continuity and vendor support, a 5-year contract to Upgrade Oracle Argus on-Prem to Oracle Safety One (SaaS) is required.

Based on the evaluation made in Project TED and later an RFI process and ITLT review (more details in the slide deck)

## Decision
Review

## Recommendations
The council members have reviewed the request asynchronously and found no members objecting to this decision.
Architecture council approves the 5-year contract to Upgrade Oracle Argus on-Prem to Oracle Safety One (SaaS)
